# What to Expect After Bariatric Surgery

Recovery looks a little different for everyone, but most patients follow a similar path.

## The first few days

- Hospital stay and early walking
- Hydration and a clear liquid diet
- Managing discomfort

## The first few weeks

- Progressing through diet phases
- Protein and vitamins
- Follow-up appointments

## Long-term

- Building consistent habits
- Monitoring labs
- Ongoing support
