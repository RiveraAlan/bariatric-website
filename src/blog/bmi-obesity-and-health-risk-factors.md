# BMI, Obesity, and Health Risk Factors

BMI is one screening tool that can help estimate weight-related risk.

## BMI categories

- Underweight
- Normal
- Overweight
- Obesity

## Important note

BMI does not capture everything. Body composition, medical history, and lab results also matter.
